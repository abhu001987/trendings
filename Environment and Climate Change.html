Environment and Climate Change

Why in News?

Environmental protection and climate change are major global concerns. Rising temperatures, pollution, and loss of biodiversity are affecting ecosystems and human life.

What is Environment?

Environment includes all living and non-living things around us such as air, water, land, plants, animals, and humans.

Major Environmental Problems

  • Climate Change: Rising global temperatures due to greenhouse gases.
  • Air Pollution: Caused by vehicles, industries, and burning fuels.
  • Water Pollution: Due to industrial waste and sewage.
  • Deforestation: Cutting of forests for agriculture and development.
  • Loss of Biodiversity: Extinction of plants and animals.

Steps Taken by India

  • Promotion of renewable energy like solar and wind.
  • National Action Plan on Climate Change (NAPCC).
  • Swachh Bharat Mission to reduce pollution.
  • Afforestation and wildlife protection programs.

Way Forward

  • Use clean and renewable energy.
  • Reduce pollution and plastic use.
  • Protect forests and wildlife.
  • Promote sustainable development.
