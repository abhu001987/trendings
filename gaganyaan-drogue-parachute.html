DRDO Conducts Gaganyaan’s Drogue Parachute Test

Why in News?

The Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) successfully conducted the test of the drogue parachute system for ISRO’s Gaganyaan mission. This test is important to ensure safe landing of astronauts during the mission.

About Gaganyaan Mission

  • Gaganyaan is India’s first human spaceflight mission.
  • It is being developed by the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO).
  • The mission aims to send Indian astronauts (Gagannauts) into space.
  • It will demonstrate India’s capability in human spaceflight.

What is a Drogue Parachute?

  • A drogue parachute is a small parachute used to slow down a spacecraft.
  • It stabilizes the capsule during re-entry into Earth’s atmosphere.
  • It helps deploy the main parachute safely.
  • It ensures safe landing of astronauts in the sea.

Role of DRDO

  • DRDO developed and tested the parachute system.
  • The test ensures reliability and safety of the crew module.
  • DRDO is supporting ISRO in human safety systems.

Importance of the Test

  • Ensures astronaut safety during landing.
  • Strengthens India’s human spaceflight capability.
  • Important step towards successful Gaganyaan mission.
  • Enhances India’s space technology and defense cooperation.

Way Forward

  • Continue testing safety systems.
  • Prepare for crewed spaceflight launch.
  • Strengthen India’s space research and innovation.
