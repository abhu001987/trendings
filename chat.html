Community Guidelines

  • Be respectful and kind to everyone.
  • No harassment, bullying, or hate speech.
  • No spam, repeated messages, or flooding the chat.
  • No sexual, explicit, or inappropriate content.
  • No illegal, harmful, or dangerous discussions.
  • Do not share personal information (yours or others).
  • No links, promotions, or self-advertising.
  • Keep conversations relevant and meaningful.
  • Avoid using offensive or abusive language.
  • Respect different opinions and viewpoints.
  • Do not impersonate others or mislead users.
  • Follow moderator or admin instructions.
  • Violating rules may result in message removal or ban.
  • Help maintain a positive and safe community.
