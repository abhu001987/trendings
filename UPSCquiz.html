UPSC Prelims Practice Quiz
1. Which one of the following is the correct definition of “Biodiversity”?
Study of plants only
Variety of living organisms in a region
Study of animals only
Study of atmosphere
Biodiversity refers to the variety of life forms including plants, animals, and microorganisms.
2. Which Article of the Constitution deals with Fundamental Rights?
Article 12 to 35
Article 36 to 51
Article 52 to 78
Article 79 to 122
Fundamental Rights are mentioned in Articles 12 to 35.
3. Which layer of Earth is responsible for plate tectonics?
Inner Core
Outer Core
Lithosphere
Atmosphere
Lithosphere is the rigid outer layer responsible for tectonic plate movement.
4. Who appoints the Chief Election Commissioner of India?
President
Prime Minister
Supreme Court
Parliament
Chief Election Commissioner is appointed by the President.
5. Which gas is mainly responsible for global warming?
Carbon dioxide
Oxygen
Nitrogen
Hydrogen
Carbon dioxide is the main greenhouse gas responsible for global warming.
